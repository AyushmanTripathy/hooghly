Hooghly Agro Products Pvt. Ltd. is the manufacturer of the crude rice bran oil and de-oiled rice bran. The company was founded in 2011.

Our attention has been paid to continuosly production technology development and quality control. Our every process of production of crude rice bran oil and de-Oiled rice bran is closely supervised and manufacture by the specialist.
